Who We Are

Our Story & Mission

Founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a Central Government-licensed, non-profit Section 8 organization headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.

We believe every individual deserves dignity, opportunity, and care. Our mission is to serve communities compassionately — through food, education, skill-building, women's empowerment, animal welfare, and environmental sustainability.

With a growing network of volunteers and corporate partners, we work across multiple states — Chhattisgarh, Delhi, Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, and Madhya Pradesh — creating measurable, lasting change.

Section 8 Registered 80G & 12A Certified CSR-1 Registered NITI Aayog Listed ISO 9001:2015

What We Do

Our Ongoing Projects

Six flagship programs address the most pressing challenges faced by underserved communities across India.

🍱
Project SEVA

Distributing meals, food packets, and clothing to homeless and underprivileged individuals — because no one should go to bed hungry.

✅ 50,000+ meals distributed

📚
Project BACHPANSHALA

Bridging educational gaps for underprivileged children through digital literacy, life skills training, and school education support.

✅ Education for hundreds of children

👩‍💼
Project UDAAN

Empowering women through skill development programs, vocational training, and pathways to financial independence and self-reliance.

✅ Women-led livelihoods created

🌿
Project PRAKRITI

Driving environmental conservation — from tree plantation drives and clean-up campaigns to sustainability education for communities.

✅ Green India, cleaner tomorrow

🐾
Project JEEV

Feeding stray animals, promoting animal welfare awareness, and advocating for the rights and safety of all living creatures.

✅ Thousands of animals fed & cared for

💼
Project VIKAS

Equipping youth with professional and digital skills through structured internship programs, enhancing employability across India.

✅ 30,000+ interns trained
